Staff Engineering Manager — Product Engineering

We are growing an engineering organization that balances delivery with sustainable team health. The staff engineering manager leads a multi-squad group, sets technical direction in partnership with staff engineers, and builds managers who scale culture and outcomes.

You will:
- Manage and develop engineering managers and senior ICs across two to three squads
- Align roadmap priorities with product and design; resolve cross-team dependencies early
- Establish hiring plans, interview loops, and leveling rubrics consistent with company values
- Coach on career growth, performance feedback, and inclusive team rituals
- Represent engineering in planning forums; communicate risks, tradeoffs, and mitigation options
- Sponsor architecture reviews for initiatives with multi-quarter blast radius
- Maintain operational excellence: incident readiness, quality gates, and predictable release cadence

Requirements:
- 10+ years in software engineering with at least 4 years people management at scale
- Experience leading managers or large teams (15+ engineers) in a product-led company
- Demonstrated success shipping complex systems while improving team engagement and retention
- Strength in organizational design: team topology, ownership boundaries, and communication flows
- Fluency in modern cloud-native stacks sufficient to challenge technical proposals
- Executive-level written and verbal communication for stakeholders outside engineering

Nice to have:
- Background in zero-to-one product bets and sunsetting legacy systems safely
- Experience with distributed teams across time zones and async decision records
- Familiarity with compliance-sensitive domains (SOC2-minded practices, audit-friendly change control)
- Prior IC career in backend or platform before transitioning to management

Leadership expectations: model psychological safety and crisp accountability, invest in manager community through peer forums and shared playbooks, and balance customer urgency with sustainable pace.
