Platform / SRE Engineer — Infrastructure Team

Our platform team keeps production dependable while enabling product teams to ship quickly. This role blends software engineering with operations: you will automate toil, harden critical paths, and define standards for observability, capacity, and secure delivery.

Responsibilities include:
- Operate and evolve Kubernetes-orchestrated workloads and supporting ingress, secrets, and config
- Own CI/CD pipelines, artifact promotion, and rollback playbooks across staging and production
- Define SLOs, error budgets, and alerting that reduce noise while catching user-impacting regressions
- Lead incident response, facilitate blameless reviews, and drive remediations that prevent recurrence
- Partner with application teams on performance, autoscaling, and cost-aware resource right-sizing
- Implement infrastructure as code with reviewed changes and documented runbooks

Requirements:
- 5+ years in platform, SRE, DevOps, or backend roles with production on-call experience
- Hands-on experience with Linux, containers, orchestration, and network fundamentals
- Proficiency in at least one IaC tool and one general-purpose language (Go, Python, or TypeScript)
- Deep familiarity with metrics, logs, traces, and dashboard design for actionable operations
- Understanding of identity, TLS, secrets management, and least-privilege access patterns
- Ability to communicate tradeoffs between velocity, reliability, and operational cost

Nice to have:
- Experience with service mesh, progressive delivery, or chaos engineering practices
- Background tuning Postgres or Redis under mixed read/write workloads
- Prior work standardizing golden paths for new services (templates, linting, security baselines)
- Contributions to internal developer portals or self-service environment provisioning

Operating model: embedded partnership with two product squads per quarter, weekly reliability review covering SLO burn and capacity forecasts, and investment time reserved for automation.
